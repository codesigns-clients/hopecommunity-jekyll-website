What we do

Nurturing confident, caring and curious children is what we do. Hope Community Nursery offers high quality childcare and education for 2 to 5 year olds in a stimulating and comfortable atmosphere ensuring all children feel included, secure and valued.

Your child matters to us

At Hope Community Nursery we consider every ‘little person’ to be a beautiful independent, fascinating and creative individual with a huge amount to offer the world.

Our Promise to you

To provide the highest standards of care and nursery education tailored to your child’s individual interests and needs. Following the Early Years Foundation Stage Curriculum and the principles of "planning in the moment".

Every child is valued, and their sense of identity and self-esteem is our priority and an essential part of their learning.
